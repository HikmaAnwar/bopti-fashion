VIRTUAL WARDROBE STYLING

Discover the ease and convenience of virtual wardrobe styling with BOPTI FITS, your personal fashion expert online. Whether you're looking to refresh your everyday look, need assistance for a special event, or want a complete wardrobe overhaul, we're here to help entirely online.


Step 1:STYLE QUIZ & PAYMENT

Help our stylists create your personalized outfits by taking a 10 question style quiz about your sizing,budget, aesthetic, and more!
After the style quiz, your Lookbook will be added to your cart to check out and place your order.

step 2: WAITING PERIOD

Once your order is processed, you may email us inspo pictures or products you already own that you would like to see styled in your lookbook.
Our stylists will start creating your Lookbook and send within the delivery time you chose!

STEP 3: RECEIVAL & DIRECT SHOPPING

Receive your Personalized Loobook via your preferred email and start shopping for your outfits directly from your Lookbook!
All styled pieces in your Lookbook will be linked, enabling you to easily click and shop.
